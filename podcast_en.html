  About WhynotTV Podcast
WhynotTV Podcast embraces the power of deep, extended conversations. Our 2-4 hour format allows for nuanced exploration of complex topics, giving guests the time to share their complete thoughts and experiences. This isn't just about AI and technology—it's about understanding the human stories behind the innovations that shape our world. Perfect for:
        - Researchers seeking in-depth technical discussions;
        - Builders looking for practical insights and real-world applications;
        - Students wanting to understand the full context of cutting-edge developments.
        - Startup founders exploring the intersection of technology and entrepreneurship.

Hosted by Tairan He, Robotics & AI PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University's Robotics Institute.
